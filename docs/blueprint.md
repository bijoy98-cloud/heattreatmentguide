# **App Name**: Heat Treatment Guide

## Core Features:

- Interactive Glossary: Searchable database of heat treatment terms, microstructures, properties, equipment, and defects.
- Calculator Module: Generate steel grades and processes with parameters such as temperature, soak times and cooling.
- AI-Enhanced Parameter Tool: AI-powered tool that recommends optimal heat treatment parameters, acting as a virtual metallurgist to suggest appropriate temperature ranges, soaking times, and cooling methods based on material composition and desired properties.
- Quiz Zone: Multiple-choice quizzes on heat treatment processes and metallurgy with progress tracking.
- Learning Hub: Curated tutorials and reference materials from the 'Heat Treatment Guide' YouTube channel by Bijoy Saha and other textbooks.
- Video Summaries: AI powered tool that watches each of the tutorial videos and generate summaries of the contents.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Steel blue (#4682B4) to convey professionalism and reliability.
- Background color: Light gray (#E5E5E5) for a clean and modern interface.
- Accent color: Gold (#FFD700) to highlight key information and interactive elements, signifying expertise.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif font with a modern look; suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use metallurgy-related icons, simple and easily recognizable.
- Clear, modular layout with distinct sections for each feature, designed for easy navigation.
- Subtle transitions between sections.
